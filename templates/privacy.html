Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account and use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Your name and email address, provided at registration.
  • Expense records you enter, including amount, category, date, and description.
  • Basic usage data such as login timestamps.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive personal data beyond what is listed above.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service:

  • To authenticate you and display your expense history.
  • To generate summaries, category breakdowns, and spending insights.
  • To communicate with you about your account if needed.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We take reasonable precautions — including access controls and encrypted connections — to protect it from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss.

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. We will process the request within 30 days.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to load typefaces. Google may log font requests as part of their standard infrastructure; no personal account data is shared with them.

We do not integrate with advertising networks, analytics platforms, or data brokers. No personal data is sold or shared with third parties.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy or would like to request access to or deletion of your data, please reach out via the contact information provided in the app.
